# Rank and Rent Compliance Guidelines

## Core Principles

This site routes inquiries to our chosen fulfillment partner for 1031 exchange advisory and property identification support. All content follows rank and rent compliance standards.

## Content Rules

### Never Claim Past Projects or Specific Clients

- Do not reference specific past projects or named clients
- Frame scenarios as examples of the type of engagement we can handle
- Use generic examples that illustrate capabilities without claiming specific outcomes

### Reference Sharing

- Invite contact to share real references upon request
- Do not display testimonials or client names without explicit permission
- References are available upon request, not displayed publicly

### Timelines and Outcomes

- Do not fabricate timelines or outcomes
- Use realistic examples based on typical exchange processes
- Acknowledge that actual results vary based on individual circumstances

### Testimonials and Reviews

- No testimonials displayed on the site
- No review stars or ratings without verified sources
- No badges or certifications without clear attribution

### Tax and Legal Disclaimers

- 1031 exchanges defer income tax on qualifying real property
- Exchanges do not remove transfer or documentary taxes
- All content is educational only, not tax, legal, or investment advice
- Users should consult with qualified tax and legal advisors

### Securities Disclaimers

- DST or TIC investments may be securities
- We do not sell securities
- We provide introductions to licensed providers only
- Include securities disclaimers when DST or TIC is mentioned

## Footer Disclosure

All pages must include the following disclosure in the footer:

"Disclosure: This site routes inquiries to our chosen fulfillment partner for 1031 exchange advisory and property identification support."

## Compliance Checklist

- [ ] No specific client names or projects referenced
- [ ] Examples framed as capability demonstrations
- [ ] References available upon request only
- [ ] No fabricated timelines or outcomes
- [ ] No testimonials or unverified reviews
- [ ] Tax and legal disclaimers included
- [ ] Securities disclaimers where applicable
- [ ] Footer disclosure present
- [ ] Educational content only, no advice
